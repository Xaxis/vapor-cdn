How it works?

By leveraging modern web technologies such as WebRTC, VCDN creates a peer-to-peer network of your site's current users. Subsequent connecting users download your site's content (Images, JavaScript, Video Streams, etc) directly from their peers.

Offsetting bandwidth from your host to VCDN and your site peers has the potential to save big money. Beyond savings, VCDN is energy efficient by diminishing the need for big data and server farms which helps the environment.
